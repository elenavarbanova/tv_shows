Actors




    Main


    Introduced in season one:
  • * María Pedraza as Marina Nunier Osuna, Guzmán's sister and love interest of Nano and Samuel.
  • * Álvaro Rico as Leopoldo "Polo" Benavent Villada, Carla's ex-boyfriend and Cayetana's love interest.
  • * Ester Expósito as Carla Rosón Caleruega, Polo's ex-girlfriend who later falls in love with Samuel.
  • * Miguel Herrán as Christian Varela Expósito, one of three transfer students.
  • * Itzan Escamilla as Samuel García Domínguez, one of three transfer students, who is the love interest of Marina and later falls for Carla.
  • * Jaime Lorente as Fernando "Nano" García Domínguez, Samuel's older brother who just got out of prison, who is also a love interest of Marina.
  • * Miguel Bernardeau as Guzmán Nunier Osuna, adopted brother of Marina, and Lu's ex-boyfriend, who falls for Nadia.
  • * Mina El Hammani as Nadia Shanaa, one of three transfer students, the daughter of Palestinian immigrants and the love interest of Guzmán
  • * Omar Ayuso as Omar Shanaa, Nadia's brother, who falls for Ander against his father's wishes.
  • * Arón Piper as Ander Muñoz, son of the principal who falls for Omar.
  • * Danna Paola as Lucrecia "Lu" Montesinos Hendrich, Guzmán's ex-girlfriend who shares an incestuous relationship with her half-brother, Valerio.

    • Introduced in season two:
  • * Jorge López as Valerio Montesinos Rojas, Lu's half-brother.
  • * Claudia Salas as Rebeka "Rebe" de Bormujo Ávalos, a rebel, wealthy girl, who has a crush on Samuel. She is different from the other wealthier students in her class as she likes to extravagantly flaunt her wealth through her clothes and jewellery.
  • * Georgina Amorós as Cayetana Grajera Pando, the daughter of a cleaning lady who lives a fraudulent lifestyle and is the love interest of Polo.

    • Introduced in season three:
  • * Leïti Sène as Malik, a love interest of Omar. He is flirtatious, and manipulative, using Nadia as a beard to get closer to Omar.
  • * Sergio Momo as Yeray, a love interest of Carla. A young wealthy student who started up his own business.
